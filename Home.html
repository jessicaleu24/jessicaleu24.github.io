Hi. I'm Jessica Leu.

Hi! I'm Jessica Leu, a PhD student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at UC Berkeley. I'm working with Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka in Mechanical Systems & Control Lab. My research interest includes robotics and human robot interactions, control and motion planning, optimization and model predictive control, mechanical design, and assistive devices.
